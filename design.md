# Blueberry Events — Luxury Event Brand Experience

For a luxury wedding + entertainment brand, the homepage should feel less like a “website” and more like an emotional cinematic experience.

The design direction for **Blueberry Events** should combine:

* Romantic luxury aesthetics
* Editorial-style layouts
* Smooth immersive storytelling
* Premium typography
* Soft motion design
* High-end hospitality/event branding energy

---

## Brand Personality

Blueberry Events should feel like:

# Animation System

## Motion Philosophy

Animations should feel:

* Slow
* Smooth
* Emotional
* Cinematic
* Expensive

Avoid:

* Fast flashy motion
* Excessive bouncing
* Overly playful transitions

---

## Recommended Motion Stack

### Framer Motion

Primary animation engine.

### GSAP

For:

* Cinematic scroll experiences
* Parallax storytelling
* Advanced timelines

### Lenis

For ultra-smooth scrolling.

---

# Animation Types

## Scroll Reveal

* Fade-up
* Blur-to-clear
* Mask reveals

## Hover Motion

* Image zoom
* Soft glow
* Floating depth

## Text Animation
